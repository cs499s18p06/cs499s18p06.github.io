Raycasting:

These tutorials were extremely helpful in creating the ShootingMechanic.cs and Target.cs scripts. The ShootingMechanics.cs script determines if an approved target is hit by the bullets (raycasts) and applies damage to that object. The target.cs script is only attached to approved targets. When an approved target is hit, that specific target’s health meter drops; upon hitting zero the target will turn red and disappear.
